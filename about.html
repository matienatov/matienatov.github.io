About Me



Hi! My name is Matie Natov. I love making beautiful and easy-to-use websites. I have eight years of experience making and creating websites, web pages, landing pages and graphic designs. I graduated from California State University Monterey Bay in 2014 with a B.S. Degree in Communication Design, Concentration in Web Design, and Business Minor.

I love working with people to collaborate with different skills, backgrounds, talents and cultures. I believe teamwork can help transform lives!

I've worked with marketing firms, start-ups, small business owners, and large corporations to design, develop, and publish amazing website experiences, both public-facing and internal-facing.

I love working in a Content Management System as much as I love hand-coding a website. (like this one!) Also, I love working independently as much as I love working with a team. I'm just passionate about web design, simplicity, and making complex things feel simple.

I'd love to hear from you! Please contact me or view my resume to learn more.
