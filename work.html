Sephora

While at Sephora, I was responsible for designing, developing and publishing assets for Sephora's internal-facing learning application, My Sephora Learning, serving 17,000 users. The app runs on a Content Management System, and the app allows certain configurations and customizations, which I led. I worked with Directors, Managers and more team players to plan and produce UX and visual design options for stakeholder review and approval.

App Icons


App Icons

Design, develop, and prepare nine variations of a mobile icon for stakeholders to review. In the end, an all white version was picked.

App Wireframes


App Wireframes

Leading the User Experience Design efforts, I was responsibel for planning and preparing wireframes, or blueprints, of how the app screens could function and navigate together for the user.

App User Flow


App User Flow

I was responsible for the app sitemap and navigation. To help prepare and plan this part of the process, I created custom user flows.
