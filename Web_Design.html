Marketing Email Design for Snow Resort

In 2014, I designed and developed a web template for a Snow Resort company. The template was first designed in Adobe Photoshop. Then, images were sliced and exported for web use. I embedded the images in a HTML and CSS web page file and optimized it for web viewing on all browsers and devices. In this image, you can see the visual layout of the email design and color theming as it matches the brand. The company logo is near the top, along with social media links, and navigation. A large graphic image spans the layout with a play button overlay that launches a marketing video when someone clicks it. Underneath the main graphic image, you will see a catchy headline and paragraph that entices readers to book with the resort at a discounted rate. This email design was published to 13,000 target users and had a 20% open rate, which is great for email advertising.
