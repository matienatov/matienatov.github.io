Logo Design

In 2020, I planned, sketched, and designed a logo for Sephora's education technology. The logo features a mobile icon, a rounded square with the platform's acronym type-set within a rounded square and a subtle drop shadow. Next to the icon, is typography of the platform's name. The letter spacing and kerning was set to make the typography really easy to read on an interface design.





Logo Design for Tech Company

In 2016, I worked with a tech company to design their website logo. The image shows a beautiful typography display of a sans-serif font. The letter spacing and kerning are set perfectly, to make the type easy to read. The Q letter has special treatment to make it look interesting. The colors break up the two words. The logo is easy to read and legible from near and far.





Logo Design for Breast Cancer Prevention Project

In 2014, I helped design and build a logo for a breast cancer prevention project. I used Adobe Illustrator to create the logo design. In this display image, you will see a standout icon of a breast cancer ribbon within a blue circle shape. Under the icon, there is typography that is set perfectly to read, "Breast Cancer Prevention Project". The logo is used for a variety of marketing purposes.





Logo Design for California Foodies Group

In 2013, I designed and built a logo for a California Foodies Group. The display image shows a logo of the california state in 3D, with a big bite taken out of the middle of the state, with crumbs left below. The logo makes an impact and is visually representative of the meaning of the group. I made this logo in college, as part of a web design project.
